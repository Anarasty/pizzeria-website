About us

Cafe "Pizzeria" is a great place to relax, because it is both a cafe and a pizzeria. This is the place where you can plunge into the world of Italian cuisine! Here you can feel the taste of real Italian pizza! "Pizzeria" is the most delicious pizza.

Come and try the pizza to any taste, salads, cold and hot appetizers, juicy steaks, hot fish and meat dishes. In addition, the facility has a separate room for billiards.
